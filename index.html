The problem

Indian students face real pressure

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Can my parents or teachers see my answers?+
No. Your answers are stored only on your own device. InnerMap does not store your data on any server. Nobody — including us — can ever see what you write. Your journal, your quiz answers, your profile — all of it belongs only to you.
Is InnerMap really free to start?+
Yes. You can complete all 3 quizzes, see your full profile, and use the private journal completely free. After your free week, you can continue the deeper growth journey for ₹99 per month — paid via UPI, GPay, PhonePe, or card. You can cancel anytime.
What if I don't know what I want — can I still use InnerMap?+
That is exactly who InnerMap is built for. Every quiz has an honest option for students who don't know yet. If you pick mostly those, InnerMap gives you a special response — not a result, but a warm, guided path to start discovering. Not knowing is the beginning, not a failure.
How is the AI weekly check-in personalised?+
When you start Week 2, InnerMap reads all your Week 1 answers and uses AI to generate 5 questions written specifically for you. If you said you love watching documentary content, your Week 2 question might ask what specifically fascinated you. No two students ever get the same questions.
Is InnerMap only for students who are confused about careers?+
InnerMap is not a career guidance tool. It is a self-discovery companion. Even students who know what they want to do will benefit — because understanding yourself deeply makes you better at everything you choose. InnerMap helps you grow as a person, not just pick a stream.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Our core promise

InnerMap is built on one fundamental principle: your answers belong only to you. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your quiz answers, journal entries, or personal reflections to any server. All data is stored locally on your own device.

2. What data we collect

InnerMap collects the absolute minimum required to provide the service:

  • Your first name (entered voluntarily at start — stored locally only)
  • Quiz answers (stored locally on your device only)
  • Journal entries (stored locally on your device only)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Razorpay — we never see your card or UPI details)

3. What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your phone number or email address
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not share any data with parents, schools, or third parties
  • We do not use cookies for advertising
  • We do not sell your data to anyone

4. AI weekly check-in

When you use the AI weekly check-in feature (Premium), your anonymised quiz answers are sent to the AI model to generate personalised questions. These are processed in real time and not stored. The AI cannot identify you personally.

5. Payments

All payments are processed by Razorpay, a licensed payment gateway regulated by the Reserve Bank of India. InnerMap never sees or stores your payment card details, UPI ID, or bank information. Razorpay's privacy policy governs their data handling.

6. Children's privacy

InnerMap is designed for users aged 13 and above. We take the privacy of young users extremely seriously. Because all data is stored locally and we collect no personal identifiable information, InnerMap is among the most privacy-safe platforms available for students in India.

7. Your rights

You can delete all your InnerMap data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or uninstalling the app. Because we store nothing on our servers, this permanently removes all your information.
